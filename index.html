Software Engineering Fellowship

Do you loves stats, puzzles and problem-solving? Think about programming or think about coding on your spare time? If you're ready for a career change into software engineering, then take the next step by joining our 12-week program!

Nimar Tech Hub runs a competitive engineering fellowship with graduates working at the best tech companies in the world. Our ultimate mission is to increase Ethiopian representation in the tech industry by developing a top-notch curriculum that creates awesome engineers and helps Ethiopian thrive in the software industry. The accelerated software development program is challenging, fun and life-changing.
